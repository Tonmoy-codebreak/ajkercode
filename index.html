Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh is a land of limitless possibilities, where resilience defines its people. It has proven time and again that creativity, determination, and innovation can transform even the most adverse conditions into opportunities for growth. Through initiatives like microfinance, we’ve seen that even the poorest can rise with the right support, becoming entrepreneurs and agents of change. The true wealth of Bangladesh lies not in its natural resources, but in its people’s spirit to dream and work for a better future. We must envision a nation free of poverty, where everyone has access to opportunity, equality, and sustainability. Together, we can build a Bangladesh that leads the way in global progress and human dignity

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
